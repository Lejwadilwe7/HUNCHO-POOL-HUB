FAQ

How do I order?

Use the shop page to add items to the cart and then checkout via WhatsApp, or press "Buy Now" on any product to message us directly with your item and quantity.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery for local deliveries and bank transfer. Details provided when we confirm your order on WhatsApp.

Do you deliver?

Yes — we deliver within Botswana. Delivery charges depend on your location; we'll confirm on WhatsApp.

Can I ask for bundles or discounts?

Yes — send us a message on WhatsApp with what you want and we’ll give bundle prices for multiple items.
